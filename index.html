Dream. Build. Launch.

You’ve got an idea. We’ll help you build it into more! The Hatchery is the place student innovators come to build a business. It’s an on-campus student incubator run by the Cal Poly Center for Innovation & Entrepreneurship (CIE) providing working space, workshops & educational tools, mentorship, and empowerment.

Workshops

Starting in the Winter Quarter, Hatchery members participate in and have access to periodic workshops including regular Friday programming on business model and customer development, prototyping and minimum viable product development, intellectual property, and more. Mentorship

The Hatchery offers student innovators access to an extensive network of entrepreneurially minded Cal Poly alumni and local community members. The program, run by the CIE’s Director of Student Innovation Programs, provides regular support from experienced entrepreneurs that helps students learn both the good and challenging from those who have already lived it themselves.

Resources

Tired of trying to reserve a fishbowl in the library for your team to meet, or telling your roommate to turn down the music while you are tucked away working in your room? The Hatchery provides an ideal place to work on campus with an open office feel and a reservable conference room. Those students actively participating in the Hatchery also receive the guidance and information necessary to make them most competitive for other CIE programs like Innovation Quest and the Summer Accelerator.
